# Inventory-Management-System-Sql
MySQL-based Inventory Management System for managing vehicles, spare parts, sales, suppliers, and generating analytical reports.

# 🚗 Inventory Management System (SQL Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project is a **Database Management System** designed for a **Four-Wheeler Inventory Business**.
It manages vehicles, spare parts, suppliers, customers, sales, and invoices efficiently.

---

## ⚙️ Features

* Vehicle Inventory Management
* Spare Parts Stock Management
* Supplier & Purchase Order Tracking
* Customer & Sales Management
* Invoice & Payment Handling
* Real-time Stock Updates
* Role-Based User Access
* Reports & Analytics

---

## 🗄️ Database Schema

### Main Tables:

* Roles
* Users
* Vehicles
* Fuel Types
* Spare Parts
* Suppliers
* Customers
* Sales
* Spare Parts Sales
* Purchase Orders
* Payments
* Stock Transactions
* Invoices

---

## 📊 SQL Reports Implemented

* 📅 Monthly Sales Report
* 🚗 Top Selling Vehicles
* ⚠️ Low Stock Alert
* 📈 Sales Trends
* 📦 Vehicle Stock Overview
* 🔧 Spare Parts Sales Analysis
* 💰 Revenue by Salesperson
* 🧾 Invoice Generation
* ⏰ Overdue Invoice Tracking

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* SQL (DDL, DML, Joins, Aggregations)
